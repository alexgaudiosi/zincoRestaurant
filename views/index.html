Welcome to ZINCO Italian Eateries, exciting local neighbourhood restaurants offering a truly authentic and informal dining experience. Sapori D'Italia means flavours of Italy, and that is exactly what you will find at our Eateries. At ZINCO we are passionate about our food and wines. Our focus is to bring a true taste of Italy to our customers in a warm and welcoming atmosphere, with careful, friendly service.

ZINCO Italian Eateries are ideal venues for a meal with family and friends. We also offer a perfect setting for larger groups celebrating birthdays, anniversaries, staff parties and other special events. Don't just take our word for it. In an interview with The Mail on Sunday, Gino D'Acampo, celebrity TV chef and winner of "I'm a Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here!" said "...My favourite local restaurant is the Italian ZINCO in Shenley Road, Borehamwood." Come and see for yourself and experience a true taste of Italy.
